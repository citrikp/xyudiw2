collaborate

Music producer, film score composer, mixer and sound designer with fifteen years of experience and an approach built on two complementary axes:

axis one

as a DJ and producer with long experience in the Brazilian and international electronic music underground circuit, including 8 years as artist-in-residence at party and label Mamba Negra, I keep close watch on what is most alive in the universe of electronic music — from baile funk and dubstep to techno. I track what renews itself constantly through the deeper internet; the ever-shifting timbres of electronic music, driven by the memetic logic of post-2010 culture in full acceleration.

axis two

From a more introspective place, I use natural and human science data as compositional tools, building complex sonic landscapes and ecosystems for narratives rooted in myth, ellipsis, and cultural specificity. The sonic ecosystems section of this site's homepage shows where this leads.

what I offer

Fifteen years' experience in music production and sound design with roots in São Paulo's underground electronic, having also performed at Draaimolen (NL), Primavera Sound (ES), Roskilde (DK), Nuits Sonores (FR) with brazilian band Teto Preto. This trajectory gave me a sonic vocabulary spanning funk mandelão, automotivo, harder-edged contemporary electronics, to the emulation of complex sonic ecosystems through careful and elegant sound design, always aligned with what is most alive in the field.

approach

I compose with analog and digital synthesizers and manipulate samples through destructive techniques, paying attention to each millisecond of a specific sound as well as having high skill with sonic collage. In a recent 2025 project, I recorded and compiled an Amazonian sound library of birds, mineral sounds, bodies of water, footsteps and insects. The idea is to produce sounds that don't yet exist with the tools described here.

where I make a difference

Cinema and series: score and sound design for narratives in ellipsis, evoking the diverse realities of Brazil. Flexibility across organic or aggressive sonic textures.

Advertising: brands with environmental and cultural identity. Current electronics — funk, aggressive beats, club sonorities.

Sonic identity: projects needing a specific, hard-to-categorise sonic colour. Sustainability, culture, technology, diversity.

Installations and new media: immersive experiences, generative sound worlds, sensory environments built from natural data and phenomena.
