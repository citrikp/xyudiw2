Mari Herzer

Who I am

Music producer, film score composer, mixer and sound designer with fifteen years of experience and an approach built on two complementary axes.

Face one

As a DJ and electronic music producer, I keep close watch on what is most alive in electronic sound — from baile funk and dubstep to experimental music. I track what renews itself constantly outside traditional channels, through the deeper internet: electronic music's ever-shifting timbres, driven by the memetic logic of post-2010 culture.

Face two

From a more introspective place, I read anthropological texts and natural science data as compositional tools — building complex sonic landscapes and ecosystems for narratives rooted in myth, ellipsis, and cultural specificity. The sonic ecosystems section of this site's homepage shows where this leads.

What I offer

Fifteen years in music production and sound design, with deep roots in São Paulo's electronic music underground — including eight years as artist-in-residence at party and label Mamba Negra. This gave me a sonic vocabulary spanning funk mandelão, automotivo, harder-edged contemporary electronics, and the emulation of complex sonic ecosystems: elegant, challenging sounds aligned with what is most vital in the field.

Approach

I compose with analog and digital synthesizers alongside extreme-to-delicate sample manipulation — including, in a recent 2025 project, an extensive Amazonian sound library of birds, mineral sounds, bodies of water, footsteps and insects — producing sounds that don't yet exist.

where I make a difference

Cinema and series: score and sound design for unconventional narratives. Flexibility across organic, electronic and hybrid textures.

Contemporary advertising: brands with environmental, cultural or territorial identity. Current electronics — funk, aggressive beats, club sonorities.

Sonic identity: projects needing a specific, hard-to-categorise sonic colour. Sustainability, culture, technology, diversity.

Installations and new media: immersive experiences, generative sound worlds, sensory environments built from natural data and phenomena.
