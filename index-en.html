Mari Herzer

collaborate

Music producer, film score composer and sound designer — drawing from unconventional readings of anthropological texts and natural science data as compositional tools.

what i offer

Fifteen years of experience in music production and sound design, with a formative presence in Sao Paulo's electronic music scene — including eight years as a live PA and DJ at Mamba Negra, one of the city's largest electronic music parties. That trajectory built an ear and a sonic vocabulary that moves naturally between funk, automotivo, aggressive contemporary electronics and the naturalistic emulation of complex sensory ecosystems — delivering, across any of these registers, elegant, challenging and contemporary sonorities.

approach

One of the starting points of my work is an unconventional reading of anthropological texts and the use of natural science data as compositional material. This produces a sonic identity that resists obvious references — and becomes a precise differentiator for projects demanding something new, whether in building sonic worlds for cinema, creating electronic textures for contemporary advertising, or emulating organic natural environments.

where i make a difference

Cinema and series: score and sound design for unconventional narratives. Flexibility across organic, electronic and hybrid textures.

Contemporary advertising: brands with environmental, cultural or territorial identity. Current electronics — funk, aggressive beats, club sonorities.

Sonic identity: projects needing a specific, hard-to-categorise sonic colour. Sustainability, culture, technology, diversity.

Installations and new media: immersive experiences, generative sound worlds, sensory environments built from natural data and phenomena.
